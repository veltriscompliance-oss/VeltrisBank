Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my transaction PIN?
Log in to your dashboard, go to Settings, and select "Change Transaction PIN". You will need to verify your identity via email OTP.
What are the daily wire limits?
For verified accounts, the daily domestic wire limit is $50,000. International wires are capped at $100,000 per day. Contact support for higher limits.
How long do deposits take to clear?
Mobile check deposits typically clear within 1-2 business days. Wire transfers received before 4 PM ET are available immediately.
